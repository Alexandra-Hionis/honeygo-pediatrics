Immunizations & Vaccines

Immunization Info

Vaccines are a safe and effective way to protect your baby from serious diseases. Immunizations have been one of the single greatest advances in preventative medicine and an essential part of well-child care. Our practice follows the immunization guidelines recommended by the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP).

    We offer:
  • COVID-19 Vaccinations
  • Seasonal Flu Vaccinations
  • RSV Vaccinations available to infants and children from birth to 24 months of age

If you have any questions or concerns about vaccines and immunizations, please call our office at 410-933-8101. For information regarding these vaccines, immunization schedules, and vaccine safety, you can also visit the AAP childhood immunizations page.
