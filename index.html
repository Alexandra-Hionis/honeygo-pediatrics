Serving the local community for over 25 years, Honeygo Pediatrics provides quality, compassionate care from birth up to 19 years old, with transition care up to 21 years old. We are a small tight knit practice specializing in care for infants, children, adolescents, and young adults. Our Services

Well Visits

Well visits are crucial to your the child's growth and development in order to find or prevent problems.

Sick Visits

Is your child feeling under the weather? Schedule a sick appointment with us today.

Annual Physical

A physical examination, or check-up, is highly recommended for children of all ages.

Immunizations and flu shots

We offer updated COVID-19 vaccinations and Flu vaccinations

School and sports physicals

Is your child trying out for a school sport? It is required that your child is certified by a healthcare provider prior to participating.

Work, School, And Travel Exams

Children should have a medical examination prior to attending school or work, or engaging in travel. All involve protecting individual children and others around them.

Newborn Care

Having a new baby can evoke excitement as well as trepidation. Trust us to help you every step of the way.

Telehealth Visits

Ask about our virtual medical services. Telemedicine is available for most illnesses.
