Sports and School Physicals

Why See The Pediatrician For A Sports Physical?

The American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) suggests making an appointment for a sports phsycial at least 6-8 weeks prior to starting a season to have some wiggle room for any additional follow-ups or new treatments. If you didn't quite make that deadline, no problem! We are more than happy to help you set up your child's next sports physical.

Important Things Examined During Sports Physicals

There are a few key things that we examine for during our sports physicals.

  • Heart health
  • Mental health
  • Unique concerns of female athletes
  • Unique concerns of disabled athletes
