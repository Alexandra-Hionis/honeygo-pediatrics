Pediatric Virtual Visits

We are excited to offer virtual medical visits designed for kids and teens. With Telehealth Visits, you and your family can connect directly with us from the comfort of your home.

Requirements

Being prepared for your child's virtual appointment will ensure that the process goes smoothly. Here are some tips to consider:

  • Access to a phone, tablet, or computer with a microphone and camera.
  • Mental health
  • Prefferably a hardwired to ensure a secure connection.
  • A smart device with access to Wifi rather than cellular service.

For additional questions or concerns, please reach out. Virtual services are by appointment only.
