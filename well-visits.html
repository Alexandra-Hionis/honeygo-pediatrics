Well Visits

Why schedule a well visit?

Well visits are the 1st Step to your child's best health. The cornerstone of pediatrics is well child care. At Honeygo Pediatrics, well visits are crucial to evaluate and monitor your child's physical, mental, and social growth and development.

It's obvious that the best course of action when your child is sick is to visit the doctor. But pediatrician visits are just as important for healthy kids. Bright Futures and the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) created a set of comprehensive health guidelines for well-child care, known as the periodicity schedule. Essentially, it's a schedule of assessments and screenings reccommended during each well visit from infancy all the way through adolescence. We suggest you try to follow this for the best preventative healthcare.

Important things examined during well visits

During a well visit we examine many factors including:

  • Growth Measurements
  • Vision and Hearing Screening
  • Nutrition and Physical Activity Aid
  • Mental Health Screening
  • Blood Work
  • Vaccinations
  • Tobacco, Drug, Alcohol, & Sex Education
